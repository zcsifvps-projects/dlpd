Introduction to Digital Literacy

Module Overview

Digital literacy is the ability to use digital technologies such as smartphones, computers, and the internet to access information, communicate, solve problems, and participate effectively in society. This module introduces learners to basic digital concepts and builds confidence in using digital tools safely and effectively.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

  • Define digital literacy and explain its importance.
  • Identify common digital devices and their uses.
  • Demonstrate basic smartphone and computer operations.
  • Navigate the internet to find information.
  • Use digital communication tools responsibly.

Understanding Digital Devices

Learning Outcomes

Participants will be able to:

  • Identify different digital devices.
  • Describe the functions of common devices.
  • Understand how digital devices support daily life.

What is a Digital Device?

A digital device is an electronic tool that helps people create, store, process, and share information.

Common Digital Devices

Device Purpose
Smartphone Communication, internet access, mobile banking, learning
Computer/Laptop Work, education, document creation, internet access
Tablet Learning, entertainment, communication

Smart TV Accessing digital content and information
