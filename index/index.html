Digital Bridges

Inclusive digital literacy training for communities across Zambia

ZCSIF works to close Zambia's digital divide and help people use technology safely and with confidence. Digital Bridges is our flagship training program, built to give learners of every background the practical skills they need to communicate, learn, work, and stay protected online.

Start Learning → About the Foundation
Why Digital Bridges

Training designed around real needs

Practical & Hands-On

Step-by-step activities and real-life examples, not just theory.

Built for Everyone

Content for every stage, from first-time smartphone users to job seekers and entrepreneurs.

Community-Centered

Extra care for women, youth, persons with disabilities, and rural communities.

Rooted in Online Safety

Backed by the same cybersecurity expertise ZCSIF brings to communities across Zambia.

The Curriculum

Eight modules, one journey

Work through the modules in order, or jump straight to the skills you need most.

Introduction to Digital Literacy

Devices, smartphones, internet basics

Online Safety & Cyber Hygiene

Passwords, scams, privacy protection

Digital Communication

Email, messaging, collaboration tools

Information & Media Literacy

Fact-checking, misinformation awareness

Digital Financial Literacy

Mobile money, digital banking, safety

Productivity & Work Readiness

Office tools, job applications, CVs

Digital Entrepreneurship

Online business, e-commerce, marketing

Responsible Digital Citizenship

Ethics, rights, inclusive participation

Digital Bridges is one part of ZCSIF's wider work protecting online rights and building cyber resilience across Zambia.
