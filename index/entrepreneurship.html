Digital Entrepreneurship

Digital technologies provide new opportunities for individuals and small businesses to reach customers, market products, and generate income. Entrepreneurs can use digital tools to improve efficiency, expand markets, and strengthen customer relationships.

This module introduces participants to practical digital entrepreneurship skills.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

  • Use social media to promote products and services.
  • Engage customers through digital platforms.
  • Utilize digital business tools.
  • Understand the basics of e-commerce.

Social Media Marketing

What is Social Media Marketing?

Using social media platforms to promote products, services, and brands.

✓ Benefits

  • Low-cost marketing
  • Wider audience reach
  • Increased customer engagement
  • Business visibility

✓ Good Practices

  • Share relevant content regularly
  • Use quality images and videos
  • Respond to customer inquiries promptly
  • Maintain a professional online presence

Online Customer Engagement

Why Customer Engagement Matters

Strong customer relationships increase trust and loyalty.

Effective Engagement Strategies

Respond Quickly to Messages

Fast replies build customer confidence.

Address Concerns Professionally

Handle complaints calmly and respectfully.

Provide Accurate Information

Avoid misleading customers about products or services.

Encourage Customer Feedback

Use feedback to improve products and service quality.

Communication Tools Accounting Apps Inventory Systems Scheduling Tools

E-Commerce Basics

What is E-Commerce?

Buying and selling goods or services online.

✓ Benefits

  • Access to wider markets
  • Increased sales opportunities
  • Convenience for customers

Key Considerations

  • Product quality
  • Secure payment methods
  • Reliable delivery systems
  • Customer service
