Responsible Digital Citizenship

Digital citizenship refers to the responsible, ethical, and respectful use of technology and digital platforms. As more people participate in online communities, it is important to understand both the opportunities and responsibilities that come with digital engagement.

This module encourages participants to become positive contributors to digital spaces while protecting their wellbeing and respecting others.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, participants will be able to:

  • Demonstrate ethical technology use.
  • Understand digital rights and responsibilities.
  • Promote inclusive online participation.
  • Practice healthy digital habits.

Ethical Technology Use

What is Ethical Technology Use?

Using technology in ways that are honest, respectful, and responsible.

Examples

  • Respecting intellectual property
  • Avoiding cyberbullying
  • Communicating respectfully
  • Using information responsibly

Ethical Principles

Honesty

Respect

Fairness

Accountability

Digital Rights and Responsibilities

Digital Rights — Users Have the Right To:

  • Access information
  • Privacy and data protection
  • Freedom of expression
  • Safe digital environments

Digital Responsibilities — Users Are Responsible For:

  • Respecting others online
  • Protecting personal information
  • Following platform rules
  • Reporting harmful content

Inclusive Online Participation

What is Digital Inclusion?

Ensuring all people can participate meaningfully in digital spaces regardless of gender, age, disability, language, location, or socioeconomic status.

Inclusive Practices

Use Respectful Language

Speak and write in ways that include and welcome everyone.

Welcome Diverse Perspectives

Value opinions and experiences different from your own.

Support Accessibility

Help make digital spaces usable for people with disabilities.

Avoid Discrimination and Harassment

Treat all community members fairly and with dignity.

Benefits

  • Stronger communities
  • Greater innovation
  • Equal opportunities
